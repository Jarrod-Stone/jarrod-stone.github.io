Brisbane DNA

Brisbane DNA explores the thoughts, opinions and life stories of 100 Brisbane residents, taking us beyond the stereotypes and statistics and revealing the heart of modern Brisbane. The exciting culmination of this city-wide quest is 100 people that not only represent a demographic profile of our city, but by generously sharing their own views, feelings and experiences, offer a dynamic picture of who is Brisbane. Brisbane is a city of over 1.1 million of whom 70% were born in Australia. But statistics can only reveal so much about us. They show where we are from, but not where we are going. They provide a record of where we live and where we work, but not how we think and feel about ourselves, our city, and our place in the world. These 100 people accurately portray the Australian Bureau of Statistics data in the categories of gender, age group, region of birth, household composition and residential location for Brisbane.
