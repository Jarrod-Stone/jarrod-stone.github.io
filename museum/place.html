Place

"Airports are fascinating places. Some areas are bustling with human activity, while others have dramatic architecture that showcases the great skies of south east Queensland." - Robert Brownhall, Brisbane artist, 2016

The everyday events, people and places of Brisbane's dynamic urban landscape are the focus of Brownhall's work. A Brisbane born artist, Brownhall has been making artworks for more than 20 years inspired by his local environment that reveal the personality of the architecture and the way people live within the city. As the first artist-in-residence at Brisbane Airporrt, Brownhall was given a rare, access-all-areas pass to create a body of works that capture moments in time at one of Australia's busiest airports. The artist-in-residence program is an extension of Brisbane Airport's long-term commitment to supporting local artists.
